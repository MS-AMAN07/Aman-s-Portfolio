Recipe for making Maggi!!

Steps:

  • Take a bowl and heat it for some time on stove
  • Now, for each packet of maggi and two cups of small glass
  • Boil the water for some time
  • When the water is heated upto required then add the maggi masala in the water
  • Cook the masala for some time
    1. Add the maggi in the boiling masala water
    2. Cook the Maggi untill its get into required posture and stickyness
  • Then serve the maggi in then bowl
  • Have your delicious maggi!!
